Frequently Asked Questions

What types of websites do you accept?

We accept all types of websites including e-commerce sites, blogs, corporate websites, portfolio sites and niche sites. The website must be active for at least 6 months and traffic and revenue data must be verifiable.

How long does it take to sell a website?

Sales time varies depending on the website's value, niche and market conditions. On average, websites sell within 30-90 days. Premium websites sell faster, while sites with very specific niches may take longer.

How do you determine the value of a website?

Our valuation process considers many factors such as traffic, revenue, growth trends, niche competition, content quality and technical aspects. We also analyze similar sales in the market to ensure proper pricing.

What information do I need to provide?

Basic information is required about your website's URL, niche, traffic data (Google Analytics), revenue reports, content quality and technical setup. The more detailed information you provide, the more accurate our evaluation will be.
